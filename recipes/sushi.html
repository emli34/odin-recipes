Recipe for Sushi

This is a recipe for sushi. There will be no need for any other sushi recipes after this one. Hope you enjoy!

Ingredients (3 servings)

  • 3 Nori Sheets
  • 1/2 a Cucumber, sliced
  • 3 Imitation Crab sticks
  • 1.5 Cups of Rice
  • 0.75 lbs of Sushi Grade Tuna, sliced

Steps to Make your Very Own Sushi

  1. Prepare all ingredients and have it all laid out (mise en place).
  2. Slightly wet bamboo mat, and lay a single sheet of nori shiny side down onto the mat.
  3. Spread a thin layer of rice onto the nori sheet leaving a cm of space at the edge away from you.
  4. Layer the ingredients onto the edge of the sheet closest to you.
  5. Begin to roll the sheet away from you, making sure to roll with the mat.
  6. Use the bamboo mat to shape the roll when you have finished rolling the sushi.
  7. Slice the sushi roll into eight even pieces and enjoy!
