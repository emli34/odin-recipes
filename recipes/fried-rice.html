Recipe for Fried Rice

This is a recipe for Fried Rice. There will be no need for any other Fried Rice recipes after this one. Hope you enjoy!

Ingredients (4 servings)

  • diced carrots
  • frozen peas
  • day old rice
  • soy sauce
  • frozen corn
  • oyster sauce
  • eggs

Steps to Make your Very Own Fried Rice

  1. cook ingredients sepreately
  2. combine the ingredients with rice and cook until ready
  3. stir in sauce ans serve
